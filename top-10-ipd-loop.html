Top 10 / Explanatory capture

Moments the case tried to escape iPD and got pulled back.

The point is not that iPD was never possible. The point is that iPD became the place where every contradiction was sent to disappear.

  1. Autosomal recessive “idiopathic” Parkinson's.
    The phrase holds genetic causation and idiopathic framing in unstable suspension.
  2. PRKN is considered, then postponed.
    Genetics enters the room, but does not interrupt finality.
  3. Levodopa is deferred with possible regret.
    The record moves toward incompletely discriminated Parkinsonism, then back to settled iPD management.
  4. Procyclidine withdrawal returns the outside-left-foot phenomenon.
    A focal foot signal responds to central medication change, yet the frame does not migrate.
  5. The foot throw is called parkinsonian.
    The record pulls the feet into the central movement disorder, then continues to route the case back through iPD.
  6. Sinemet replaces Kemadrin without a retrospective diagnostic reckoning.
    The delayed discriminator becomes treatment, not correction.
  7. Severe foot pain creates a 2023 crisis.
    The original signal returns as urgent harm, yet is translated back into the old frame.
  8. Misalignment is openly recorded.
    The clinician's belief that everything is explainable by Parkinson's is set against the patient's reasonable concern that the feet are separate.
  9. Pain is not Parkinson's, but Parkinson's medication relieves it.
    The loop tightens: the frame both denies and absorbs the symptom.
  10. “We have known” replaces “we suspected.”
    The later defence retrospectively hardens what the earlier record repeatedly destabilised.
The record wandered, but it did not migrate.
