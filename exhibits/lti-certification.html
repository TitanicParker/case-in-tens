The LTI Certification

The point where a provisional hypothesis became portable administrative reliance.

CertificationRelianceHardening

What it is

The Long Term Illness certification is a statutory and administrative artefact. It does not merely describe a clinical view. It gives the label usefulness in systems outside the consultation.

Why it matters

Certification changes the burden of correction. A working diagnosis can evolve. A certified diagnosis travels. It recruits future records, pharmacy logic, GP assumptions, complaint handling, and patient identity.

What it shows

It shows suspicion becoming reliance before the unresolved elements had been clearly resolved or marked as provisional. That is why the form belongs in the centre of the hall, not at the edge.

The institution stopped merely suspecting and began relying.
