The March 2025 Rebuttal

The later defence document that preserves the frame while acknowledging facts that keep the feet diagnostically material.

RebuttalPainFrame preservation

What it is

This exhibit concerns later correspondence defending the diagnostic frame while addressing pain, medication response, and the continuing role of Parkinson's treatment.

Why it matters

It matters because a defence can reveal the instability it is trying to contain. If pain is said not to be directly Parkinsonian, but Parkinson's medication is acknowledged to relieve the experience of pain, the foot problem remains diagnostically alive.

What it shows

It shows explanatory capture. The frame can absorb pain as magnified experience, absorb medication response as non-motor fluctuation, and absorb contradiction as management complexity, without allowing any of those facts to displace the frame.

Everything returns to the same harbour.
