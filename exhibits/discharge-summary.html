The Discharge Summary

The artefact that records incompleteness while performing completion.

ClosureRed flagsHierarchy

What it is

The discharge summary is the principal closure document. It carries the diagnosis forward while also preserving qualification, atypicality, treatment caution, and unresolved features.

Why it matters

The document matters because it contains its own challenge. It does not simply omit uncertainty. It documents enough uncertainty to make unqualified finality unsafe unless the bridge to closure is clearly explained.

What it shows

It shows a hierarchy problem. Certainty governs the heading and the administrative effect. Uncertainty remains in the body of the narrative. The result is a record that can later be treated as settled while still containing the reasons settlement was premature.

Uncertainty preserved as prose. Certainty exercised as power.
