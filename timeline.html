Hinge chronology

The sequence is the evidence.

This is not a dense chronology. It is the pathway of foreclosure: entry, translation, withholding, certification, confirmation, afterlife.

Entry Through the Feet

The referral fixes the problem neurology accepted: abnormal gait, neurological signs, function, and foot-centred pathology.

Translation Failure

The formal diagnostic problem becomes more abstract than the bodily signal that brought the patient into the institution.

Withheld Discriminator

Dopaminergic therapy is deferred. The diagnostic consequence of that deferral is not allowed to govern finality.

Certification Before Completion

The LTI form gives the diagnosis administrative portability while material inquiry remains incomplete.

Protest Before Closure

The patient reasserts the feet in ordinary language. The protest does not become the governing record.

Confirmation After Closure

The DAT scan arrives as supportive evidence after the frame had already begun to rely on itself.

Longitudinal Return of the Feet

Years of notes keep rediscovering the same focal signal: pain, posture, gait, medication response, and explanatory instability.

Governance Inherits Closure

The complaint and data pathways encounter the closed frame as if it were neutral background.

Genetic Confirmation

Later causal clarification does not create the original ordering defect. It illuminates why the earlier warnings mattered.
