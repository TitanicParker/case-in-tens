The twelve essays.

Each essay is an exhibit plaque expanded into argument. The sequence moves from discharge artefact to master ordering rule to final defence trap.

Essay 1

The Document That Knew Too Much

Thesis: the discharge summary records the red flags and then closes over them.

If Levodopa Was Withheld, Certainty Had to Be Withheld Too
