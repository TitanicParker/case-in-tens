Essay 10

Finality Before Inquiry

The master ordering essay: the case as sequence inversion, not hindsight complaint.

Master ruleOrderingFinality
Finality must follow inquiry. It must not precede it.

Thesis

The cleanest way to understand the case is not as a dispute about whether a diagnosis could be suspected. It is a dispute about order. Suspicion may be reasonable. Closure is a separate act.

Medicine may act provisionally. Institutions may not behave as though a question has been settled while the process required to settle it remains incomplete.

The sequence

The case entered through a foot-centred neurological problem. A Parkinsonian frame was recognised. Atypicality was recorded. A discriminator was withheld. Possible regret and specialist limitation were recorded. The feet remained unresolved. Finality and certification proceeded anyway.

That order is the case.

The rule in practice

Permissible conduct includes working diagnosis, caution, further testing, specialist review, and explicit uncertainty. The impermissible move is durable administrative finality while the initiating problem remains unresolved and material discriminators remain unused.

Do not close what you have not completed. Do not certify what you have not sufficiently discriminated.
