Essay 11

The Drift from iPD Back to iPD

On recurring destabilisation, explanatory capture, and the return of contradiction to the same frame.

iPDExplanatory captureLoop
Not that iPD was never possible. That iPD became the place where every contradiction was sent to disappear.

Thesis

The problem is not that an idiopathic Parkinson's frame was unavailable. It plainly was. The problem is that the record repeatedly encountered reasons to treat that frame as incomplete, provisional, or unstable, and then returned those reasons to the same explanatory centre.

The record wandered. It did not migrate.

The loop

Genetic possibility is considered, then postponed. Dystonic foot signs appear, then are absorbed. Medication response matters, then is translated back into the existing frame. Severe foot pain returns, then is contained. The patient's concern is called reasonable, then subordinated to the prior explanation.

Each detour appears to open the case. Each return makes the old frame harder to displace.

The governing danger

A diagnostic frame becomes unsafe when no fact is allowed to become the fact that changes it. If every contradiction can be called expression, complication, magnification, coincidence, or management detail, then the process is no longer genuinely open.

The conclusion explains the evidence, and the evidence is translated to preserve the conclusion.
