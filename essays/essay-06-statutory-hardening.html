Essay 6

The Statutory Hardening of a Provisional Hypothesis

On certification as the point where suspicion became reliance.

LTIRelianceAdministrative fact
The certification is the moment the institution stopped merely suspecting and began relying.

Thesis

Certification matters because it changes the status of the diagnosis. A working impression can remain local and revisable. A certified condition travels. It enters systems, records, prescribing logic, future correspondence, and later governance.

That is why this form is not secondary. It is the hinge where clinical uncertainty becomes administrative fact.

The problem

The surrounding record still contained active uncertainty: unresolved entry signal, atypical features, non-use of a key discriminator, possible genetic disease, and a recorded limit of specialist ownership. The certification did not carry that uncertainty with equal force.

The gap between a qualified clinical record and an unqualified administrative fact is the problem.

The safer route

Support and provisionality could have coexisted. The record could have made clear that certification was being used for practical access while classification remained under review. That would have allowed help without foreclosure.

Without that qualification, the form hardened the label before the inquiry had earned that hardness.
