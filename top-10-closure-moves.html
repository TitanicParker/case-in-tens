Top 10 / Mechanics

Closure moves.

Premature finality rarely announces itself. It works through small moves: translation, demotion, certification, confirmation, omission, and later reclassification.

  1. Translate the presenting problem.
    Move from foot-centred gait failure to abstract Parkinsonian classification.
  2. Preserve the red flags as prose.
    Keep the warning signs visible enough to prove diligence.
  3. Deny the red flags governing force.
    Let none of them delay closure.
  4. Defer the discriminator.
    Withhold the intervention that might have clarified the frame.
  5. Keep certainty moving.
    Do not let the withheld discriminator make the diagnosis provisional.
  6. Record a limitation.
    Say the case requires a jurisdiction you do not occupy.
  7. Do not alter the pathway.
    Let the limitation sit as insulation rather than escalation.
  8. Certify the hypothesis.
    Turn clinical suspicion into administrative reliance.
  9. Use later evidence as reassurance.
    Let confirmation after closure appear to justify the earlier closure.
  10. Call later challenge clinical disagreement.
    Convert sequence, accuracy, and governance questions back into clinical judgment.
The system did not merely close. It made closure harder to undo.
