Five minute orientation

Start with the order.

The case is not best understood as a dispute over whether a diagnosis could be suspected. It is a dispute over whether finality was exercised before inquiry had completed.

The rule

Finality must follow inquiry. It must not precede it. A system may be uncertain, cautious, and provisional. It may not convert unresolved inquiry into durable administrative fact while the material questions remain open.

The entry point

The case entered through gait, function, and the feet. The referral did not simply ask whether Parkinson's disease was present. It asked neurology to explain why this person could not walk properly, given what was happening in his feet.

The hinge

Dopaminergic therapy was not merely treatment. In this case it also carried discriminating value. If levodopa was withheld for caution, certainty had to be withheld too.

The hardening

Certification changes the state of the record. A suspicion can evolve. A certified diagnosis travels. It recruits pharmacy, GP, complaint, governance, and future clinical reliance.

The afterlife

After premature finality, review can continue without inquiry continuing. A patient can be followed for years inside a closed premise. A contradiction repeatedly managed is not a contradiction resolved.
